1. Executive Summary
Organizations collect massive amounts of operational, financial, sales, marketing, and customer data. However, most decisions still rely on manually prepared dashboards, spreadsheets, and analysts. Traditional Business Intelligence platforms excel at visualization but rarely explain why trends occur or what actions should be taken.The AI Business Analyst Platform aims to bridge this gap by combining automated data profiling, exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical reasoning, and Large Language Models (LLMs) to produce executive-ready insights from uploaded datasets.
2. Vision
Build an AI-powered Business Analyst that understands enterprise datasets, automatically discovers meaningful insights, explains business trends in natural language, recommends actions, and enables conversational analytics.
3. Mission
Reduce the time from raw business data to executive decision from hours or days to a few minutes.
4. Problem Statement
Business users often struggle with:• Cleaning datasets• Creating dashboards• Writing SQL queries• Interpreting charts• Explaining trends to stakeholdersThe platform should automate these repetitive analytical tasks while keeping humans in control of final decisions.
5. Business Opportunity
Small businesses, startups and enterprises increasingly adopt AI assistants, yet many still lack an AI-native analytics solution that combines automatic analysis, recommendations and conversational interaction in one workflow.
6. Product Vision
Core workflow:1. User uploads a dataset (CSV/Excel).2. System validates and profiles the data.3. Automatic EDA and KPI generation.4. Charts and anomaly detection.5. LLM generates executive insights.6. User asks follow-up questions.7. System recommends business actions.

10. Market Gap
Existing solutions generally excel at either visualization or conversational querying. Few combine automatic dataset understanding, EDA, insight generation, recommendations, and conversational analytics into a unified product.
