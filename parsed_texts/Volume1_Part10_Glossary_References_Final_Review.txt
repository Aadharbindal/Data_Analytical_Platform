Project Assumptions
Primary data source is structured tabular data (CSV/Excel).
Users seek business insights rather than raw statistical outputs.
Deterministic analytics precede LLM reasoning.
Enterprise-scale integrations are deferred beyond Version 1.

Transition to Volume 2
Volume 2 will translate business requirements into an engineering blueprint. It will define the complete system architecture, backend services, AI pipeline, database schema, API contracts, deployment strategy and module interactions. This document becomes the implementation reference for developers and AI-assisted coding tools.
Executive Summary of Volume 1
Volume 1 established why the AI Business Analyst Platform should exist, who it serves, how it differentiates itself, what business problems it solves and the requirements for a successful MVP. The product vision centers on transforming uploaded business datasets into trustworthy, explainable and actionable insights through a combination of deterministic analytics and LLM-based reasoning.
