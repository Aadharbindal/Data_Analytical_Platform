7. Reliability Practices
Use exponential backoff for transient failures.
Implement circuit breakers for external LLM providers.
Retry idempotent background jobs.
Gracefully degrade optional AI features if providers are unavailable.
Record metrics and logs for every critical operation.
8. AntiGravity Master Prompt
Generate a production-grade performance layer for the AI Business Analyst Platform. Implement Redis caching, asynchronous workers, background job processing, rate limiting, health endpoints, metrics collection, structured logging, retry policies, circuit breakers, query optimization utilities, performance profiling and automated load tests. Keep the architecture modular and compatible with Docker and future Kubernetes deployment.
