6. Hallucination Prevention
Never invent KPIs not computed by the analytics engine.
Reference supporting metrics in every major insight.
Reject unsupported causal claims.
Return 'insufficient evidence' when confidence is low.
Separate observations from recommendations.
7. Recommendation Generation
Recommendations should combine deterministic rules and LLM reasoning. Rules identify measurable business events (e.g., revenue decline >10%), while the LLM explains likely implications and proposes next actions with clear assumptions.
