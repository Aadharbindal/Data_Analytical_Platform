6. MVP vs Enterprise Evolution
MVP focuses on structured dataset upload, automatic analytics, AI-generated insights and executive reporting. Enterprise editions extend this with connectors, RBAC, scheduling, monitoring, forecasting, multi-agent orchestration and organization-wide governance.
7. AntiGravity Development Guidance
Generate modules incrementally rather than the entire application.
Treat each service as an independent milestone.
Use the Engineering Design Specification as the source of truth.
Validate generated code against documented interfaces.
Maintain architectural consistency across generated modules.

9. Long-Term Vision
The long-term objective is to evolve the platform into an AI Business Operating System capable of continuously monitoring enterprise data, proactively identifying opportunities and risks, coordinating analytical agents and supporting strategic decision-making across multiple business domains.
