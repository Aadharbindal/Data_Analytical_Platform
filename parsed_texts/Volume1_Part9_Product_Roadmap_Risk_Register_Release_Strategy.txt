Purpose
This chapter defines the phased delivery strategy for the AI Business Analyst Platform. It outlines milestones, release objectives, risks, mitigation strategies and success measures that will guide implementation from MVP to an enterprise-grade platform.
Development Philosophy
Adopt an iterative delivery model. Each release must provide usable business value while creating a stable foundation for future AI capabilities. Version 1 focuses on correctness and usability rather than feature volume.

Release Strategy
Release 1 targets internal validation using representative business datasets. Feedback should be collected from business analysts, finance teams and technical reviewers before expanding to additional domains. Every release must improve insight quality, explainability and user experience rather than simply increasing the number of features.
Future Vision
After a stable MVP, the platform should evolve into an AI Business Operating System capable of connecting to enterprise data sources, continuously monitoring KPIs, generating scheduled executive reports and proactively identifying business risks and opportunities.
